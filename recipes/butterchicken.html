Butter Chicken

Butter chicken : A great dish to have with friends!

Steps

  • Marinate the chicken
    1. Combine cubed chicken thighs or breasts (about 1.5 lbs) with ½ cup plain yogurt, 1 tsp lemon juice, 1 clove minced garlic, grated ginger (to taste), a pinch of turmeric, ½ tsp red chili powder, ½ tsp garam masala, and ½ tsp salt.
    2. Mix thoroughly and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes, or preferably overnight for the best flavor and tenderness.
  • Make the sauce
    1. In a large pan, heat 1.5-2 tbsp ghee or butter.
    2. Add ½ chopped red onion and 1 tsp cumin seeds and cook on medium heat until the onions soften.
    3. Add 2 cloves minced garlic and 1 tsp minced ginger and cook until fragrant.
    4. Stir in 2/3 cup crushed canned tomatoes (or pureed fresh tomatoes), a pinch of turmeric, 1 tsp ground cumin, 1 tsp garam masala, ½ tsp allspice, 1 tsp red chili powder, and ½ tsp salt.
    5. Simmer for 4-5 minutes until the spices meld and the tomato paste thickens.
    6. Transfer the mixture to a blender with 2/3 cup water and blend until smooth.
  • Cook the chicken
    1. In the same pan, add the marinated chicken and cook until lightly browned or the outside is cooked through.
  • Finish the curry
    1. Pour the blended sauce back into the pan with the chicken.
    2. Add an additional splash of water if needed to adjust the consistency.
    3. Simmer for 3-4 minutes.
    4. Stir in 2 tbsp cream and 2 tbsp kasoori methi (dried fenugreek leaves). Allow the sauce to thicken slightly.
  • Serve
    1. Serve hot with basmati rice or naan bread.
    2. Garnish with fresh cilantro if desired.
