Chicken Pakora

An image of Chicken Pakora dish made by a chef of Green Valley Cafe

Ingredients

Chicken

  • Boneless chicken thighs or breast

Flours

  • Gram flour
  • Rice flour
  • Cornstarch

Spices and Seasonings

  • Ginger
  • Chili powder
  • Garam masala
  • Cumin powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Salt

Other Ingredients

  • Onions/li>
  • Green chilies
  • Curry leaves
  • Coriander leaves
  • Lemon juice
  • Water
  • Egg
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Add the following ingredients to a large mixing bowl
    • 6 tablespoon besan (gram flour) (¼ cup + 2 tbsps)
    • 3 tablespoon rice flour or cornstarch
    • ⅓ teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)
    • ¼ teaspoon turmeric (haldi)
    • ¾ to 1 teaspoon Kashmiri red chili powder (reduce for kids)
    • ¾ to 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • ¾ tablespoon ginger garlic paste or crushed
